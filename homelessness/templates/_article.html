311 complaints in S.F.

By Aaron Williams and Joaquin Palomino

Since 2011, San Francisco’s 311 portal has recorded more than 48,000 complaints about encampments, human waste and needles on the streets. The total volume of such calls has not only increased, but also their proportion, suggesting that residents are growing more and more frustrated. While encampment complaints are closely related to homelessness, the correlation is weaker for needles and human waste.

About the data

Needle and human waste complaints were mapped using 311 data from the beginning of 2011 to Oct. 17 of this year. Encampment complaints come from service requests made to Public Works through both 311 and internal sources. 2011’s data only goes back to early July. Source: Public Works and 311
